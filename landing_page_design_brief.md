### Typography
```

- H1 (Hero): 48-56px, Bold (700), letter-spacing: -0.02em

- H3 (Subsection): 24-28px, SemiBold (600)
- Body: 18-20px, Regular (400), line-height: 1.6
- Small: 14-16px, Regular (400)

```

## Interaction & Micro-animations

**Page Load**:
- Fade in hero (300ms ease-out)
- No heavy animations (respect 45+ users)

**Scroll Behavior**:
- Smooth scroll to sections
- Subtle parallax on hero gradient (10% slower than scroll)

**Interactive Demo**:
- Typing animation: Character-by-character (50ms delay)
- Brief preview: Slide up from bottom (400ms ease-out)
- Loading state: Subtle pulse, no spinners

**Hover States**:
- Buttons: Transform scale(1.02), shadow lift
- Cards: Border color shift to Indigo
- Links: Underline fade-in (200ms)

**Mobile Behavior**:
- Hamburger menu: Slide from right (smooth, 300ms)
- Touch targets: Minimum 44px height
- No hover states on mobile
